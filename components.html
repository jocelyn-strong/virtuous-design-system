Bio

I am an Austin-based UX/UI and Graphic Designer. Before entering the design field, I earned a BA in Psychology from UT Austin while working in hospital settings. Through those experiences I knew I wanted to help people. I’ve been working as a Graphic Designer now for 3 years at a print production company in Austin while studying User Experience Design at the local community college. I’m excited to work in an area of design where I can merge my experiences in Psychology and Graphic Design to help solve design problems and improve the user experience.

Beyond Vision

Beyond Vision is a platform for building virtual-reality and augmented-reality experiences. They are focused on acquiring and onboarding enterprise-level clients. Their image is sleek, high-end, and reliable.

Virtuous Design System

I chose to name my design system "Virtuous Design System" because the word virtual is derived from Latin virtus. Virtuous is defined as having high moral standards. Beyond Vision is focused on acquiring enterprise-level clients who will have high standards for the product and want to ensure the product is reliable so I chose a name that portrays these qualities and meets the standards of those clients.

Bio

I am an Austin-based UX/UI and Graphic Designer. Before entering the design field, I earned a BA in Psychology from UT Austin while working in hospital settings. Through those experiences I knew I wanted to help people. I’ve been working as a Graphic Designer now for 3 years at a print production company in Austin while studying User Experience Design at the local community college. I’m excited to work in an area of design where I can merge my experiences in Psychology and Graphic Design to help solve design problems and improve the user experience.
